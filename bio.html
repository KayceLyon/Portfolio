My Coding Journey

My interest began at a young age while watching my mother navigate our old Windows '93 computer via DOS. I started learning how to code in Java in high school, and continued at the University of Texas at Austin. After leaving, I began learning on my own through online courses and the General Assembly Software Immersive bootcamp. One of the joys of programming is knowing that the learning process never stops.
